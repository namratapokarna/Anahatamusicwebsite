Who we are

Mrs Dhanashree Kulkarni is a talented vocalist who nurtures a deep passion for Indian Classical Music. She is an ardent singer who believes learning is a continual process. She has spent a large part of her life on music education and propagation.

what we do

Anahata Music Atelier is an institution based in Pune, devoted to education and training in Indian Classical Music. Founded and directed by Mrs Dhanashree Kulkarni, Anahata Music Atelier has been training music lovers since 1996
