Shastriya Sangeet Course

Reasons for Choosing Shastriya Sangeet Course

classical music makes you an all rounder and confident musician and teaches you to comfortably express yourself.


Course details



  • Age Group- 6 years and above

  • Duration- 4 Months

  • Every session- 1 hr


Who is this course for?


  • -Those who want to learn Shastriya sangeet from the core

  • -Those who want to appear for the examinations conducted by Gandharva Mahavidyalaya (ABGMV)

  • -People who want to learn the base of the music and his core history

  • -Candidates who want to prepare themselves as a performer.


What will you learn?


  • -The beauty of Shastriya Sangeet

  • -The Performing art of the ragas

  • -Opportunity to sing on stage

  • -Guidance of Ragas and the respective Taal.


Personal Session


  • -Guidance will be provided personally for Exams and the performing art of ragas

  • -12 sessions per month

  • -Certificate will be provided after completion of course


Group Session


  • -Personal attention should be given while training

  • -12 sessions per month

  • -Certificate will be provided after completion of course
