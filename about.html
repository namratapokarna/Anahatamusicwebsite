Shree Vishal Gandratwar

Tabla And Side Rhythm

Mr. Vishal Gandratwar is a tabla master. He holds a master degree in music. For the past ten years, in various classical concerts, he has been playing the tabla with well-known artist Shree Hridaynathji Mangeshkar. He has also worked with honourable artists like Ashaji Bhosale, Ushaji Mangeshkar and many other artists.
